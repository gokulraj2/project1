Chicken Fried Rice

    Ingredients needed

  • chicken
  • rice
  • egg
  • carrot
  • cabbage
  • capsicum

    Procedure:

    1. Keep the pan in the stove and put some oil
    2. Add onions & fry for some time
    3. And then add cabbage ,carrot , fried chicken
    4. Fry for some time after that add the boiled rice
    5. put salt ,chilli powder and soya sawce as needed
    6. at last add chopped onion leaves and serve it hot
